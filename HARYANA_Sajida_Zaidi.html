Sajida Zaidi Profile Details

Sajida Zaidi was a distinguished Urdu poet, critic, and educationist whose profound verses and intellectual contributions enriched Urdu literature. Born in Aligarh, she was deeply associated with Aligarh Muslim University, where she served as a professor. Her poetry, marked by emotional depth and social consciousness, addressed themes of human relationships, identity, and resilience, earning her a revered place in modern Urdu poetry.

Sajida Zaidi was born in 1925 in Aligarh, Uttar Pradesh, into a scholarly family with a deep appreciation for literature and education. She pursued her education at Aligarh Muslim University (AMU), where she excelled in Urdu literature and developed a passion for poetry. Her academic environment at AMU, a hub of intellectual and cultural activity, shaped her poetic and critical sensibilities, laying the foundation for her distinguished career.
