Early Life and Education

Sri Sri was born on April 30, 1910, in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, to a Telugu Brahmin family. His father, Venkata Ramana Rao, was a school teacher. Showing an early aptitude for literature, Sri Sri was largely self-educated but briefly attended Madras Christian College. Influenced by Marxist ideology and the socio-political climate of colonial India, he began writing poetry that challenged societal norms and championed the working class.
