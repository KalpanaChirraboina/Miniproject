Desmond L Kharmawphlang Profile Details

Desmond L Kharmawphlang, born in 1961 in Shillong, Meghalaya, was a distinguished Khasi poet, folklorist, and scholar whose work enriched Khasi literature and cultural studies. A professor at North-Eastern Hill University, he founded the Centre for Cultural and Creative Studies, preserving Khasi oral traditions. His poetry, blending Khasi folklore with modern themes, and his scholarly contributions made him a pivotal figure in Northeast Indian literature.
